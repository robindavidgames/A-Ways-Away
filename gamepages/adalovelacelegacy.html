Ada Lovelace: Legacy of the Analytical Engine


EXTRA! EXTRA! MR X STRIKES AGAIN!

In a clear statement that no museum is safe from his mechanisms, Mr X has pilfered from yet another British institute of history...


Ada Lovelace: Legacy of the Analytical Engine is a solitaire roll-and-write legacy game, following our protagonist, Ada Lovelace, as she chases the elusive Mr X around the globe. Over the course of 9 levels, players will examine crime scenes, upgrade their skills, and try to thwart Mr X's evil plans.

A legacy game is one in which player-choices permanently change the game components and possible outcomes. In Ada Lovelace: Legacy of the Analytical Engine, players will choose upgrades for their character and unlock bonuses and other abilities, depending on how well they do in a level or by completing certain objectives.

Fans of Ada Lovelace: Consulting Mathematician will know what to expect, while enjoying the new and unique levels, story, and legacy elements in the game. For everybody else, this is a perfect jumping-on point!

This is a print-at-home game and can be purchased from itch.io.
