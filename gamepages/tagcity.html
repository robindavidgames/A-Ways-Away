Tag City

Tag City is a deluxe roll-and-write game published in 2018 by Runes Editions.

In the game, players are rival graffiti artists, looking to cover as much of the city as possible. They do this through a fun and interactive drafting mechanism and by placing tetromino pieces on a city-grid.

You can read a full design diary of the game here.

Tag City also served as inspiration for my other games, Ada Locelace: Consulting Mathematician and Ada Lovelace: Legacy of the Analytical Engine.
